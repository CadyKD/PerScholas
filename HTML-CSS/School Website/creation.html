Character Creation

Character creation is an open-ended process where you come up with a character based on any story idea you want. There are many different worlds/universes/realms that you can invent a character for, and there are no real limitations for your characters, but if you want your characters to interact with other writers characters, then they should fit the same world and be of similar 'power.' The only characters that should have godlike powers are NPC's.

Characters can be submitted from the Join page.
